The Venue

Throughout the retreat, sunshine floods through floor-to-ceiling windows. The house is clean, comfortable and beautifully presented with outdoor terraces to enjoy the glorious weather and delicious mountain air.

With a spacious layout and intelligent design, all guests will enjoy ample space and privacy throughout their stay, benefitting from personal bathroom facilities and access to a near infrared lamp sauna.

On summer days, the house is kept refreshingly cool with traditional, schist walls. Winter evenings are warm and cosy beside the open fire - snuggle up on the sofa with friends and family or a good book and a cuppa’.
