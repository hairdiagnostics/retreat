Holidays At The Retreat

The holiday retreat supports every aspect of Nutritional Balancing, allowing individuals, couples and families alike to take a truly stress-free and restorative break.

The venue is fully equipped to facilitate health and healing, with air purifiers (ioniser/ozonators) throughout, near infrared saunas and everything needed for your daily detox protocols. Further supports include a portable heat lamp, chi machine and well stocked kitchens with stainless steel cookware and juicers.

We can fill the fridge at your request or guide you in sourcing local ingredients for yourself. Bodywork sessions can also be booked during your stay, subject to availability, with both Craniosacral Therapy and Visceral Manipulation treatments on offer.

Whatever you’re looking for from your holiday, this is perfect spot to make the most of your trip and enjoy the sunshine, safely in the knowledge that all your programme needs are covered. If you have any questions or you would like to know more, please complete our contact form or send us an email at retreat@hairdiagnostics.com.
