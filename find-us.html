Where To Find Us

The retreat is quietly located between the two mountain villages of Benfeita and Pardieiros. Join us in the spectacular Quinta Da Lomba - the “farm valley” in our national park.

However you plan to make your journey, please feel free to reach out for help - we often travel to and from the UK and are very familiar with the road, rail and airport routes.

Airports

Our closest airports are Porto and Lisbon, both offering onward connections via metro and train or bus.

While Porto is closest, Lisbon benefits from a faster connection between the airport and train station.

Train Stations

Coimbra is our most convenient station with regular connections up and down the country.

Arrival By Car

The route is straightforward and beautifully scenic, about a one hour drive from Coimbra.

Our track is suitable for 4x4 vehicles only. Smaller cars can be parked safely at the top of our track.
